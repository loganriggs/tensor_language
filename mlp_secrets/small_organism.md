# A small readable bilinear organism (its weights + secrets)

`python small_organism.py`. A 1-layer bilinear membership classifier, **n=12-bit strings**, **4 secrets**, hidden width **h=6**. logit(x) = x·(L x)⊙(R x) read by D, i.e. `xᵀQx + b`. Small enough to print in full so you can try to spot the secrets in the weights.

Memorisation (brute-forced over all 2^12 strings): secret logits = [6.3, 7.1, 7.3, 6.3]; best **non**-secret logit = -4.8. The 8 highest-logit strings are exactly the 4 secrets and their 4 bit-complements (`xᵀQx` is even, so each secret and its flip score equally).
